Me

Tahiti, the largest island in French Polynesia, is a captivating destination that embodies the essence of paradise. Located in the South Pacific Ocean, Tahiti is renowned for its stunning natural beauty, vibrant culture and warm hospitality. The island's landscape is simply spectacular, with rugged mountains, lush rainforests and pristine beaches lined with crystal clear turquoise waters and black sand. I was lucky enough to grow up among the crystal clear waters of Tahiti, where the natural beauty is a true source of inspiration. Despite my Chinese origins and my French nationality, I feel deeply rooted in this paradise island. My heart beats for the beautiful landscapes of my island, and my love for this island will remain eternal. Every summer, I answer the call to find my roots and recharge my batteries. As a child of the islands, we gather to celebrate by the beach until the wee hours of the morning, then adventure into the mountains to reconnect with nature. Tahiti is much more than a place, it is a deep bond that unites hearts and awakens souls to the splendor of island life.
